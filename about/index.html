About

Nino Wicaksono is a passionate software developer, he has experience working in a wide range of industries from Banking, Consumer Electronic, Semiconductor, Automotive, to Storage Technology. Currently, his professional domain is in the firmware security. More on the professional records in his linkedin profile.
He works with C language and comfortable with batch and shell scripting, Python, and C++. He uses Vim to boost his productivity.

He ships various products for consumers and enterprises. He bootstrapping from the idea, concept to end product into customer’s hands. He is happy when he sees his products are creating value and solving problems for customers. He is a team player and he enjoys coaching and developing a strong team.

Reading is what he does to kill time, mostly technical works and trends in software development, financial and investment market dynamics, business and operational management, macroeconomy. Not in a particular order.

He will create a startup, as he feels that he has what it takes and cannot wait to create one.

Background

Nino Wicaksono was born and raised in Indonesia, he was born in Kalimantan and spends his childhood in Central Java, however, East Java is his hometown.
He got his undergraduate in System Information from Bina Nusantara University, Indonesia, and Master of Business Administration from University of Manchester, UK.

In his leisure time he likes to do sports, he runs 5 km daily during workdays. Weekends are weekends, a time to enjoy life and watching football matches, he is a long time supporter of AS Roma, where he mostly suffer from the game outcome.

He and Wulan raised 2 beautiful kids in Singapore, a place they call home. They have traveled to many countries, especially during the school holiday.
